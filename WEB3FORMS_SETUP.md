# Contact Form Setup Instructions

Your contact form is now ready! Follow these simple steps to activate it:

## Step 1: Get Your Free Access Key

1. Go to: **https://web3forms.com**
2. Enter your email address (where you want to receive contact form submissions)
3. Click "Get Your Access Key"
4. Check your email and copy the access key

## Step 2: Add Your Access Key

Open `contact.html` and find this line (around line 60):
```html
<input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">
```

Replace `YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE` with your actual key:
```html
<input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="abc123-your-real-key-here">
```

## Step 3: Test It!

1. Open your website
2. Go to the Contact page
3. Fill out the form with test data
4. Click Submit
5. Check your email for the submission!

## Troubleshooting

**Not receiving emails?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify the access key is correct
- Make sure you verified your email with Web3Forms

**Form shows error?**
- Check browser console (F12) for errors
- Verify internet connection
- Make sure all form fields are filled

## What Happens When Someone Submits?

1. User fills form on your website
2. Form data sent securely to Web3Forms
3. You receive email with:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Service interested in
   - Message
4. You can reply directly to the customer's email
